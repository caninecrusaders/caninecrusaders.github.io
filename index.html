Welcome! We are the Kettle Falls High School Canine Crusaders, or First Robotics Competition Team #4980. Last year, as a rookie team, we were awarded the Rookie Inspiration Award at the Central Washington University District Event, and the Rookie All Star Award at the Eastern Washington University District event. This year, however, we hope that we can achieve even more, as we are a team striving for ongoing success. Although the upcoming events are going to be rather competitive, we advocate good sportsmanship across our team. Win or lose, we vow to show good sportsmanship, fight a decent fight, and we come home from the competitions with the knowledge that operated at our utmost potential.
